About Mentor001

Mentor001 is a social organization that aim to bring together Mentors from different parts of Africa for a single goal; to nature Mentees. Mentees are matched with Mentors that have interests as their own. Thereafter, Mentoring process begin and lasts for a period of at least three months. During this period, the involved parties can arrange to meet at our offices at Swahilipot Hub. We have ample indoor and outdoor space facing the ocean.

How it works

Register

Fill the registration form by clicking on the get started of signup button and follow the prompt. Only relevant information for matching are taken together with a personality test.

Find Mentor or Mentee

Once you are registered, you can login and find a mentor by clicking the find a match button and going through the list of possible mentors. The list returned is based on your interest and personality.

Connect

Once you have found a mentor you like, click on the connect button by their names and add a simple note to personalize your request for mentorship. You can begin your mentoring process with your mentor now.
